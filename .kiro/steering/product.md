# Product Overview

AkÄ±n Kombi is a comprehensive service management system for HVAC (heating, ventilation, and air conditioning) service companies. The system manages the complete lifecycle of service operations including customer management, technician scheduling, inventory tracking, financial operations, and customer communications.

## Core Platforms

- **Desktop Application (Electron)**: Windows desktop app for office staff and managers with full administrative capabilities
- **Backend API (NestJS)**: RESTful API server handling all business logic and data management
- **Mobile App (Android)**: Technician-focused mobile application for field service operations
- **Customer Portal**: Web-based portal for customers to track services and manage appointments

## Key Features

- Customer and device management with full service history
- Kanban-style service workflow management
- Calendar-based appointment scheduling
- Technician performance tracking and route optimization
- Parts inventory management with automatic reordering
- Financial management including invoicing and payment tracking
- Multi-channel communication (SMS, Email, WhatsApp)
- Comprehensive reporting and analytics
- Offline-first mobile app for technicians
- AI-powered route optimization using Google Maps and Gemini APIs

## Business Context

The system is designed for Turkish HVAC service companies (language: Turkish). It handles both residential and commercial customers, manages warranty tracking, and supports multiple payment methods common in Turkey (cash, credit card, bank transfer).
